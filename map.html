To Add a Place to the Map

Click on the icon on the right of the screen to activate the drawing tool, then click on the map to add your point.
Once you've drawn a point, you'll be prompted to provide a name for your place and given space to share a personal story about your experience or memories of that place.
When you're done, click the Save button. All submissions to the map are anonymous.
